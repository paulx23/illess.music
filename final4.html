About ILLESS

ILLESS

ILLESS is more than a name — it's a movement. Born from the grind, powered by passion, and driven by the need to speak truth through rhythm.

Hailing from Kenya, ILLESS brings a unique blend of authentic storytelling and captivating beats that resonate with audiences worldwide. Every track tells a story, every vibe hits home.

Step into the sound and feel something real. With influences ranging from traditional African rhythms to modern hip-hop, ILLESS creates a sound that's both fresh and deeply rooted.
